Detailed Breakdown

Battery system ‚Äî Nova Pro wins completely

The Nova Pro's hot-swap battery system is genuinely unique. You get two batteries ‚Äî one in the headset, one charging in the base station. When one dies, you swap in under 10 seconds. Effectively infinite battery life. The Sony H9 has 32 hours which is good, but you will eventually have a dead headset at an inconvenient moment. For serious gaming, the Nova Pro's system is a meaningful advantage.

ANC ‚Äî Sony H9 wins

Sony's noise cancellation technology is best-in-class even on their consumer headphones. The INZONE H9 carries this over ‚Äî its ANC is noticeably stronger than the Nova Pro's. If you're in a noisy environment (shared flat, open office, family home), the H9 will block more sound.

PS5 integration ‚Äî INZONE H9 wins decisively

The Sony INZONE H9 connects directly to PS5 via USB and enables 360 Spatial Sound ‚Äî Sony's full 3D audio implementation that takes advantage of the PS5's Tempest Engine. This is the headset Sony designed for their own console. The Nova Pro works with PS5 but doesn't get the same deep integration.

Dual device connectivity ‚Äî Nova Pro wins

The Nova Pro connects to PC via 2.4GHz dongle and a console via optical simultaneously, with instant audio mixing between both sources. You can hear Discord on PC and game audio from PS5 at the same time. No other headset at this price does this as seamlessly.

Sound quality for gaming

Both are excellent for gaming audio. The Nova Pro has a slightly flatter, more neutral tuning which is better for competitive play (footstep detection). The Sony H9 has a warmer, more bass-heavy profile that many gamers prefer for immersive single-player experiences. Neither is objectively better ‚Äî they suit different preferences.

Our Verdict

If you're a PC-primary gamer or a multi-platform gamer who uses PC and console simultaneously, the Nova Pro is the better headset ‚Äî the hot-swap battery and dual-source connectivity are genuinely class-leading features. If you're primarily on PS5 and want the best integration and strongest ANC, the Sony INZONE H9 at ~¬£90 less is the smarter buy.
