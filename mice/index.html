Buying Guide

Sensor Quality

Modern gaming mice use optical sensors. The best include PixArt PAW3395 (Lamzu), Razer Focus Pro (Viper V3, Naga V2), and Logitech HERO 2 (Superlight 2). These provide flawless tracking with no acceleration, angle snapping, or jitter at any DPI setting.

DPI Explained

DPI (dots per inch) measures sensitivity. Higher isn't always better. Most pros use 400-1600 DPI. Modern sensors like the Focus Pro 35K and HERO 2 32K can handle extreme DPI settings, but accuracy at lower DPI matters more than maximum specs.

Weight Matters

Lightweight mice (under 70g) allow faster movements and reduce arm fatigue. The Razer Viper V3 Pro (54g) and Lamzu Atlantis Mini (51g) are perfect for low-sensitivity FPS players who make large swipes.

Mid-weight mice (70-100g) like the Logitech G PRO X SUPERLIGHT 2 (60g) and G305 (99g) provide balance between speed and control.

MMO mice (100g+) like the Razer Naga V2 Pro are heavier due to extra buttons, but the added functionality is essential for MMO/MOBA gameplay.

Grip Styles

Palm grip: Full hand contact. Best with ergonomic shapes like the Razer Viper V3 Pro.

Claw grip: Arched fingers, palm contact at base. Works well with the Lamzu Atlantis Mini Pro and Logitech Superlight 2.

Fingertip grip: Only fingertips touch. The Lamzu Atlantis Mini Pro's compact 51g design is ideal for this grip.

Wired vs Wireless

Modern wireless mice have zero input lag thanks to LIGHTSPEED (Logitech) and HyperSpeed (Razer) technology. Battery life ranges from 95-250 hours depending on model. Wireless is now the standard for high-end gaming mice.

Polling Rate

Standard polling is 1000Hz (1ms). The Razer Viper V3 Pro supports 8000Hz (0.125ms) with the HyperPolling Wireless Dongle, and the Lamzu Atlantis Mini supports 4000Hz. Higher polling rates reduce latency but drain battery faster.

MMO Mice Features

The Razer Naga V2 Pro includes three swappable side plates: 2-button (FPS), 6-button (MOBA), and 12-button (MMO). This versatility makes it perfect for players who switch between game genres.
