What to Look For

Lumbar support is the most important factor for back health during long sessions. Look for adjustable lumbar (not just a cushion), 4D armrests that adjust height, depth, and angle, and a seat depth that lets you sit with your back against the rest while your feet reach the floor. Materials: Leather vs Mesh vs Fabric
