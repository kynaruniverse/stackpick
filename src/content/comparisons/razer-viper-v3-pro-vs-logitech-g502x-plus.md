## Introduction

This is a clash of philosophies. The Viper V3 Pro is a stripped-back racing car designed for one thing: winning FPS gunfights. The G502 X Plus is a luxury SUV, packed with buttons, RGB, and a legendary infinite scroll wheel. Your choice depends entirely on whether you value 'performance' or 'utility'.

## Verdict

Choose the Viper V3 Pro for pure performance. Choose the G502 X Plus for the best daily driving experience.
