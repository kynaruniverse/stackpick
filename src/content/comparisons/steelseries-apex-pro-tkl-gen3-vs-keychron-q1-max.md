## Introduction

The Apex Pro TKL Gen 3 is built for speed, featuring Hall Effect sensors that allow for Rapid Trigger. The Keychron Q1 Max is built for 'thock'—a heavy aluminium chassis, multiple layers of acoustic foam, and a superior typing experience. One is a weapon, the other is an instrument.

## Verdict

Competitive gamers should buy the SteelSeries. Enthusiasts and office workers should buy the Keychron.
