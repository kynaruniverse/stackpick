Buying Guide

What to Look For

Driver Size: Larger drivers (40-50mm) generally provide better bass and overall sound quality. Most gaming headsets use 40mm or 50mm drivers.

Wired vs Wireless: Wired headsets have zero latency and don't need charging. Wireless offers freedom of movement but adds cost and requires battery management.

Microphone Quality: Look for noise-cancelling or detachable mics. Discord/TeamSpeak certified mics ensure clear communication.

Comfort: Memory foam ear cups and adjustable headbands are essential for long gaming sessions. Lightweight designs (under 300g) reduce neck strain.
