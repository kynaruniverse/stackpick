About Stack Pick

Honest gaming gear recommendations for UK gamers

Who We Are

Stack Pick is a UK-based gaming gear recommendation site. We help gamers build their perfect setup by curating the best headsets, keyboards, mice, monitors, and chairs available in the UK market.

We focus on real-world performance, UK pricing, and actual availability - no US-centric recommendations or products you can't actually buy.

Our Testing Process

Every product we recommend goes through hands-on testing:

Headsets: Tested in CS2, Valorant, and Warzone for sound quality and mic clarity. We wear each headset for 4+ hour sessions to assess comfort.

Keyboards: Tested in competitive games and typing scenarios. We measure actuation force, assess build quality, and use each keyboard for 40+ hours.

Mice: Tested across FPS, MMO, and MOBA games. We measure sensor accuracy, click latency, and durability. Each mouse gets 30+ hours of testing.

Monitors: We test input lag, response times, color accuracy, and real gaming performance across multiple genres.

Chairs: We sit in each chair for full 8-hour days over multiple weeks, assessing comfort, build quality, and ergonomic support.

Why Trust Us

We're gamers who understand what matters: performance, value, and UK availability. We don't recommend products we wouldn't buy ourselves.
