About Stack Pick

Honest gaming gear recommendations for UK gamers

Who We Are

Stack Pick is a UK-based gaming gear recommendation site. We help gamers build their perfect setup by curating the best headsets, keyboards, mice, monitors, and chairs available in the UK market.

We focus on real-world performance, UK pricing, and actual availability - no US-centric recommendations or products you can't actually buy.

Our Research Process

Every product we recommend is carefully researched and curated based on multiple factors:

Expert Reviews: We analyze professional reviews from trusted tech publications, YouTube reviewers, and industry experts to understand real-world performance.

User Feedback: We study thousands of verified purchase reviews on Amazon, Reddit gaming communities, and tech forums to identify consistent patterns in quality, durability, and value.

Specifications Analysis: We compare technical specs like driver size (headsets), actuation force (keyboards), DPI ranges (mice), refresh rates (monitors), and ergonomic features (chairs) to ensure products meet gaming standards.

UK Market Research: We verify pricing, availability, and warranty support specifically for UK buyers. Products are only recommended if they're readily available and competitively priced in the UK market.

Value Assessment: We evaluate price-to-performance ratios across budget, mid-range, and premium segments to ensure every recommendation offers genuine value.

How We Select Products

Our curation process focuses on:

Performance: Does it deliver the features gamers actually need? We prioritize products with proven performance in competitive and casual gaming scenarios.

Build Quality: Is it built to last? We research durability based on long-term user experiences and known reliability issues.

UK Availability: Can you actually buy it in the UK at a fair price? We filter out products that are US-only, overpriced due to import costs, or chronically out of stock.

Real-World Value: Would we recommend this to a friend? Every product must offer clear value for its price bracket.

Why Trust Us

We're gamers who understand what matters: performance, value, and UK availability. We research extensively so you don't have to wade through hundreds of options and contradictory reviews.

All prices are in GBP. All products are available in the UK. All recommendations are based on thorough research and genuine assessment of value.
