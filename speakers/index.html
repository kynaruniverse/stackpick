Common Questions

Are gaming speakers or a gaming headset better?

Headsets are better for competitive gaming ‚Äî directional audio and microphone in one unit, no sound leaking to others. Speakers are better for casual gaming, music, and a relaxed listening experience. Many gamers use both: headset for competitive sessions, speakers for single-player or background music.

What is the best gaming speaker setup for a desk?

For most gamers, a 2.0 or 2.1 desktop speaker system is ideal. The Logitech Z623 (2.1 with subwoofer) is the long-standing recommendation for gaming bass and volume. For pure audio quality, bookshelf speakers like the Edifier R1280T are better value than gaming-branded equivalents at similar prices.

Do I need a DAC or amplifier for desktop speakers?

For active (powered) speakers like the Edifier R1280T or Logitech Z623, no ‚Äî they have built-in amplifiers and connect directly to your PC via 3.5mm or RCA. Passive (unpowered) bookshelf speakers require a separate amplifier. For gaming use, active speakers are the practical choice.
