How the Guides Work

Each guide covers a complete desk setup at a fixed budget — not just one peripheral. We pick one item per category (mouse, keyboard, headset, monitor, chair) and explain exactly why it earns its place at that price point.

All prices are checked weekly on Amazon UK. We only list items that are currently in stock with Prime delivery. If a price rises significantly or stock dries up, we update the guide.

Every affiliate link goes directly to the product on Amazon UK. We earn a small commission if you buy — this funds the research. There are no sponsored slots or paid placements.

Price Transparency

The budget on each guide is what you'd spend buying everything in one go on Amazon UK at time of writing. Prices fluctuate — we check and update weekly. Last updated: February 2026.
