2026 Buying Guide: Glossy, Mini-LED & OLED

The "Glossy" Revolution

One of the biggest shifts in 2026 has been the move toward **Glossy WOLED** panels, like the ASUS ROG Strix listed above. Traditional matte coatings can make OLED blacks look slightly "grey" in lit rooms. Glossy panels maintain that infinite contrast and make colors "pop," though they do require more light control in your room.

Mini-LED: The OLED Alternative

If you work in a bright room or fear OLED burn-in, **Mini-LED** is now the standard. Models like the AOC Q27G3XMN offer HDR1000, meaning they get twice as bright as most OLEDs. For digital artists and HDR movie fans, the 1152 dimming zones on the MSI MAG ensure deep blacks without the risk of permanent image retention.

What is Dual-Mode?

New for this year, monitors like the MSI MAG and the LG UltraGear series feature **Dual-Mode**. This allows you to run in 4K for "pretty" single-player games, then switch to 1080p at a much higher refresh rate (320Hz+) for competitive shooters like Valorant or CS2.
